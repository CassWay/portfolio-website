About Me

Husband, Father, Security Manager, Student of Opportunities,
Lambda School Part-time Web Development

After 20 Years in the Private Security Industry in various Management positions, a change was needed to explore a new Career Path.

Background

Husband to an evolving Photographer, Father to 6 emboldened teenagers, and passionate in every desire to grow, learn and experience.

In the 1990(s) during a time when our legal system needed an “attitude adjustment”, I sought a career in Law Enforcement to bring back the prestige and realign with a positive image. Fate redirected me as I progressed through the Academy. An opportunity presented itself, and eventually interrupted my Course. A JOB to gain experience within the Field and partner with local Agencies, interact positively with the Public, and provide Beer and Gas Money for weekend frivolities.

The rollercoaster of working as a Bouncer, Security Guard, and Event Staff lead to an unintentional career spanning 2 decades. I excelled at the tasks and situations (while having fun), and became the person the general public truly trusted & respected and would seek out First to avoid having to deal with that or those “…(insert genital reference) Cop(s) …”

I climbed the ranks, shared experiences, trained new hires, and evolved into Management with numerous Companies, all at slightly above average pay for the industry. Each produced a broken handshake deal for higher wages once a particular objective was completed, despite my reputation.

I have had the pleasure to interact with some very talented domestic and international Clients (Mexico to Japan), ranging from Prom-Queen Exotic Dancers, College & NFL Teams, Americas Next Top Model, Consulado De Mexico, multiple large brand name Farms and Farming Communities, Oil Companies, Cattle Companies, Dairy, Produce, and Meat Production Facilities, popular Musicians, open air street Festivals, and private parties.

I recently (within the last year) had my promised Salary replaced with a base equivalent wage (with limitations on benefits), due to the actions (and inactions) of another whom I have never met or interacted with AND not directly connected with my jobsite, I was not consulted and not given the option to be part of the decision making. Subsequently she was Terminated relatively 1- 2 months later.

I have endured for the past year, maintaining my reputation and Professionalism, supporting my Wife in her Business ventures, raising responsible and respectable Children, and continuing my employment with the same (referenced) Company … until now as I explore educational options and New Career focus at Lambda School.
